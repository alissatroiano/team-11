Escape the Death Star

You've been captured by Darth Vader! Now you're a prisoner on the Death Star. Use your Jedi Padawan knowledge to find your way out!

how to play

  • Click the play button to start the game
  • Search the ship for mini games
  • Complete the games to unlock the door
  • Once all the padlocks above the door have changed colour, the door will open and you can escape
